Health and Work Performance Questionnaire (HPQ)

1. On a scale from 0 to 10 where 0 is the worst job performance anyone could have at your job and 10 is the performance of a top worker, how would you rate the usual performance of most workers in a job similar to yours?


2. Using the same 0-to-10 scale, how would you rate your usual job performance over the past year or two?


3. Using the same 0-to-10 scale, how would you rate your overall job performance on the days you worked during the past 6 weeks?


4. How many days/shifts have you missed over the past 6 weeks due to sickness absence
