Autonomy and Competence in Technology Adoption Questionnaire (ACTA)
There are many reasons people start using a technology. Consider how true each of these is for you. Then, rate your agreement with the following statements:

I decided to try SleepFix for the following reasons:
1. Other people want me to use it.
2. I expect it will be interesting to use.
3. I believe it can improve my life.
4. It will help me do something that is important to me.
5. I want others to know I use it.
6. I would feel bad about myself if I didn't try it.
7. I think it will be enjoyable
8. I am required to use it (eg. by my job, school, research study)
9. I feel confident that I’ll be able to use it effectively
10. It will be easy for me to use.
