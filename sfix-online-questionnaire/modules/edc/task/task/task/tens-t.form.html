Technology-based Experience of Need Satisfaction – (TENS-task)
Instructions
Reflect on your experience using the SleepFix app and rate your agreement with the following statements

1. I feel confident in my ability to keep to my sleep schedule.
2. SleepFix lets me keep to my sleep schedule in my own way.
3. I believe it’s worthwhile to keep to my sleep schedule.
4. I feel pressured to keep to my sleep schedule.
5. It’s easy to keep to my sleep schedule.
