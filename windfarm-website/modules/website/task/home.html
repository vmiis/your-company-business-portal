DO WIND FARMS CAUSE HEALTH
EFFECTS?
To help answer this question, we are seeking volunteers for a unique, important and exciting research study Medical Research was awarded by the National Health and Medical Research Council (NHMRC) to conduct two research studies investigating into the health effects of Wind Farms. This is a commonly debated topic and with very little research done in this field.

Please help participate in our world first research study
THE LABORATORY STUDY
Come to our Sleep Labs

We will be studying the effects on sleep and health over 3 separate weekends
Participants will be exposed between wind turbine noise (Infrasound), traffic and silence over each weekend visit.
Participants will be tested on their sleep, cognition,hearing and cardiovascular health.

THE HOME STUDY
Participate in your home

We will install speakers in your bedroom that deliver either infrasound, which is a sound that cannot be heard by the human ear, or no sound. These speakers will not produce audible sounds.
The type of speaker we install will be selected at random and you will not know what type of speaker is installed until the study ends.
We will visit you in your home at the beginning of the research study and also 3 months and 6 months later to conduct some tests. The range of tests will measure your sleep, heart health, emotional wellbeing and your hearing, balance and senses.
We will also ask you to have a blood test.
