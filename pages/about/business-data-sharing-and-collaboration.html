Business data sharing and collaboration

Facebook services let people be linked together. Linkedin services let professional people be linked together. There are many other similar services but most of them help people share “unstructured data”, like photo, comments, files etc. I am keep thinking that is there any possibility to help business owners to link or share their business data together? One example may be like this. If I am running a business and have a database that contains client’s information like name, phone number and home address, how can I share these information with a delivery company the people over there do not need to re-entry these data into their tracking database system, and also how they can let me to see the tracking information about my clients. If there is a company that provides a business data hosting service and both my company and the delivery company use this service, definitely this will become possible. I am sure this type of services or a new “Facebook” company will become available in the future. In order to provide a data hosting services with the functions we mentioned above, we must think of how to organize the data so we can apply a very flexible data reference permission controls. I have spend many years to build a system that let people to share “structured data” together, so as to make the business data sharing and collaboration between different business become more efficient. I am happy to demonstrate this idea if you have interest and to show what I have done.
