DO WIND FARMS CAUSE HEALTH
EFFECTS?
There have been conflicting reports about whether wind farms affect the health of people who live nearby. To help answer this question the National Health and Medical Research Council (NHMRC) has funded this world first research study to be conducted by our team at the Woolcock Institute of Medical Research. ​ We are currently seeking volunteers for two different research studies.

Click here to contact the research team
THE LABORATORY STUDY
Come to our Sleep Labs

We will be studying the effects on sleep and health over 3 separate weekends.

Participants will be exposed between wind turbine noise (Infrasound), traffic and silence over each weekend visit.

All visits at the Woolcock Institute of Medical Research, Glebe, Sydney






THE HOME STUDY
Participate in your home

We will install speakers in your bedroom that deliver either infrasound, which is a sound that cannot be heard by the human ear, or no sound. These speakers will not produce audible sounds.

The type of speaker we install will be selected at random and you will not know what type of speaker is installed until the study end.

We will visit you in your home at the beginning of the research study and also 3 months and 6 months later to conduct some tests. The range of tests will measure your sleep, heart health, emotional wellbeing and your hearing, balance and senses. We will also ask you to have a blood test.

Currently recruiting in Camden, Narrellan, and Campbelltown Areas
